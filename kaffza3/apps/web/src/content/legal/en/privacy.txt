1. Introduction
Jawaharat Al Shahba Modern LLC (“Kaffza”, “the Platform”, “we”, “us”) provides an integrated e-commerce platform that enables merchants to build online stores and enables customers to browse and purchase products securely.
This Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect personal data. It applies to all users of the Platform, including merchants, customers, and visitors.
By using Kaffza, you acknowledge that you have read and agreed to this Policy.

2. Data We Collect
A. Registration Data
• Full name

• Phone number (required for OTP verification)
• Password (encrypted; never stored in plain text)
• Account type (merchant / customer)
• Preferred language

B. Merchant Data
• Store information (name, subdomain, description, logo)
• Commercial registration and official documents
• Banking details for payouts (IBAN, account number, bank name)
• Product, inventory, and pricing data

C. Customer Data
• Shipping and delivery address

D. Automatically Collected Data

3. How We Use Your Data
• To activate and verify your account using OTP
• To process orders and payments through the Escrow system
• To send notifications about orders, shipping, and wallet activity
• To protect the Platform from fraud and manage disputes
• To improve user experience and Platform services
• To comply with legal and regulatory requirements in Oman
• To communicate with you about your account or orders

4. Sharing Data with Third Parties
We do not sell your personal data. We may share data only in the following cases:
• Thawani Pay: to process payments securely (PCI-DSS aligned)
• Jeena shipping: to deliver orders and provide shipment tracking
• Amazon Web Services (AWS): to host data on servers in the Bahrain region
• Legal/regulatory authorities: only upon a valid official legal request
• The relevant merchant: order details necessary to fulfill the purchase

5. Escrow & Payment Protection
Kaffza uses an integrated Escrow system:
• Payment amounts are held by the Platform upon successful payment
• Funds are released to the merchant when the customer confirms receipt
• If not confirmed, funds are released automatically after a period based on merchant trust level
• Customers may open a dispute during the holding period to request a refund in documented cases

6. Data Security
• Password hashing using bcrypt
• JWT tokens with defined expiry and token rotation
• HttpOnly cookies for web sessions
• HTTPS encryption for all communications
• Refresh tokens stored in Redis and revoked immediately on logout
• Audit log for sensitive operations
• Rate limiting to reduce brute-force attacks
