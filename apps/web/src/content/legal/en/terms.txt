5. Escrow System
Kaffza operates an integrated Escrow system:
• Payment amounts are held by the Platform upon successful payment.
• Funds are released to the merchant once the customer confirms receipt.
• If the customer does not confirm, funds are released automatically based on merchant trust level:
  - New merchants (first 3 orders): 14 days from delivery confirmation.
  - Standard merchants: 7 days from delivery confirmation.
  - Trusted merchants (50+ orders and 4.5+ rating): 3 days from delivery confirmation.
• Minimum withdrawal request: OMR 10.

6. Disputes
In case of a dispute between customer and merchant:
• The customer may open an official dispute only during the escrow holding period.

• Both parties provide evidence and documents to Kaffza.
• Kaffza issues a binding decision within 7 business days after evidence is complete.
• Kaffza’s decision is final regarding fund release or refund.
